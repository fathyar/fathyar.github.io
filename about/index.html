About This Blog

Hi, welcome to my blog! This is a little bit of background behind this blog.

I’ve always wanted to have a place where I can document and manage some of stuff I’ve learned and discovered. This way I can easily review and refer back to them in case I need them in the future.

Instead of just keeping it to myself, I’ve also wanted to share the knowledge that I have gained throughout my learning journey so that I hope other people can also benefit from it.

One of the things that won’t decrease if you share them is knowledge. Contrary to what we might think of material things that if we share them with others we would lose them, that is not the case with knowledge. We don’t “lose” knowledge when we share it with others.

So, those are some backgrounds behind the existence of this blog.

Another thing that motivated me to make this possible is an essay I’ve just recently discovered1 entitled ‘Learn in public’ which I also recommend you to read if you haven’t already.

Oh, one more thing. Psst! Guess what? I think I kind of suck at writing—and explaining things. I know they—the thoughts and ideas—are there, but are just like twisted threads that need to be unraveled. So, the reason, I think this blog is a great way to practice exercising my brain, better organizing my thoughts and sharpening my writing skill.

Happy reading!
